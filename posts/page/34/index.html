karo classic pecan pie

3 eggs, slightly beaten 1 cup Karo Light or Dark Corn Syrup 1 cup sugar 2 tablespoons of butter or margarine, melted 1 teaspoon of vanilla 1 1/4 cups pecans 1 (9-inch) frozen deep dish or unbaked pie crust Preheat oven and a cookie sheet to 350° F. In medium bowl combine first five ingredients. Stir in pecans. Pour into frozen pie crust. Bake on cookie sheet 50 to 55 minutes or until filling is firm 2 inches from edge....

custard pie

4 eggs 1 pint of sweet milk 1 heaping tablespoon of flour ½ teaspoon of vanilla Beat eggs, just enough to blend. Add sugar, flour and milk. Add flavoring. Bake in crust, not too fast. Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.

butterscotch pie

2 eggs 1 ½ cups of brown sugar 1 ½ cups of milk 2 tablespoons of butter 4 tablespoons of flour Add flavoring. Cook in double boiler. 1 ½ cup of brown sugar 2 cups of milk ½ cup of butter 4 egg yolks 4 level tablespoons of flour 2 teaspoons of vanilla Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.

sweet potato pie

3 well beaten eggs 1 pint of finely mashed sweet potatoes 2 cups of sugar 1 cup of carnation cream ½ cup of butter flavor with lemon juice and a little nutmeg. Makes two pies. Bake in crust as custard pies. Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.

chess pie

1 cup of sugar 1 egg ½ cup of butter 2 tablespoons of sweet cream Cook in double broiler on stove until thick. Pour in uncooked crust and bake slowly. Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.
