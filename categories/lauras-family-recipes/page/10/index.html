sweet potato pie

3 well beaten eggs 1 pint of finely mashed sweet potatoes 2 cups of sugar 1 cup of carnation cream ½ cup of butter flavor with lemon juice and a little nutmeg. Makes two pies. Bake in crust as custard pies. Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.

chess pie

1 cup of sugar 1 egg ½ cup of butter 2 tablespoons of sweet cream Cook in double broiler on stove until thick. Pour in uncooked crust and bake slowly. Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.
