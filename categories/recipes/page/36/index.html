sweet potato casserole

3 cups of mashed sweet potatoes 1/2 stick of butter 3/4 cup of milk 1 teaspoon of vanilla 1 eggs well beaten 1/2 cup of sugar Mix together. Top with 1/3 cup of brown sugar 1/2 cup of butter 1/2 cup of pecans Mix topping and sprinkle over sweet potatoes. Bake at 325° F for one hour. Recipe courtesy of Laura’s maternal grandmother, Nana.
